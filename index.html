About Me

I'm Anup Barman, a passionate competitive programmer and a Computer Science and Engineering student at Daffodil International University. My journey in programming began with curiosity and quickly grew into a deep passion for solving challenging problems and building a strong foundation in algorithms and data structures.

I have represented my university as an ICPC Dhaka Regionalist 2024, and have been a problem setter for flagship contests like Takeoff and Unlock the Algorithm. I love sharing knowledge and helping others grow in the programming community.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, collaborating on projects, and capturing memories from my competitive programming journey. My goal is to keep learning, growing, and contributing to the tech world.
